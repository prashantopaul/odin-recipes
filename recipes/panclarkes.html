Panclarkes

Short Stack of Panclarkes

Start mixing eggs, flour, milk, cinnamon, nutmeg in a bowl. Once right conistency, add to pan in batches to make panclarkes. Flip when bubbles start to rise in center. Plate panclarkes. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • milk
  • flour
  • cinnamon
  • nutmeg

Steps

  1. mix dry ingredients
  2. add wet ingredients and mix
  3. pour into hot frying pan in batches
  4. flip when bubbling in center
  5. plate and enjoy
